Recognition and Awards

In the past three years, our NGO has achieved remarkable milestones under the leadership of Ms. Rajni Joshi. Our efforts have been recognized widely, reflecting the positive impact we have made in various spheres:
Community Empowerment: We have successfully implemented numerous community empowerment programs, enabling individuals to become self-reliant and economically independent. Our vocational training and skill development workshops have opened new avenues for many.
Educational Initiatives: Our educational initiatives have reached thousands of underprivileged children, providing them with access to quality education. Through scholarships, after-school programs, and digital learning tools, we have significantly improved literacy rates and educational outcomes.
Environmental Sustainability: We have embraced environmental sustainability as a core value, implementing eco-friendly practices and raising awareness about environmental conservation. Our tree plantation drives, waste management initiatives, and sustainable development projects have made a lasting impact on our environment.
Ms. Rajni Joshi's visionary leadership and unwavering dedication have been the driving forces behind these accomplishments. Her passion for social change and her ability to inspire others have been crucial in mobilizing resources and creating a positive impact.

Naari Ratan Samman

We are immensely proud to announce that our esteemed founder, Ms. Rajni Joshi, has been honored with the prestigious Naari Ratan Samman award. This award is a testament to her unwavering dedication, tireless efforts, and significant contributions to our community through her work with PRJ Care Foundation.

Ms. Rajni Joshi has always been a beacon of hope and inspiration, leading our organization with vision and compassion. Her commitment to empowering the underprivileged and championing women's rights has transformed countless lives and brought about meaningful change.

Met SDM

In our ongoing commitment to environmental sustainability, Ms. Rajni Joshi recently met with the Sub-Divisional Magistrate (SDM) of Dwarka, Mr. Shalesh Kumar, to discuss collaborative efforts to promote eco-friendly practices and sustainable development in the region. This meeting marks a significant step forward in our mission to foster a greener and healthier environment.

Ms. Rajni Joshi emphasized the critical need for community involvement and support from local authorities to achieve long-term environmental goals. Her meeting with Mr. Shalesh Kumar reflects our proactive approach to building partnerships and leveraging collective efforts to create a positive impact on the environment.

Teacher's Day Celebration


  • Teachers' Day is an occasion dedicated to recognizing and
    appreciating the invaluable contributions of teachers in
    shaping the future of the nation.In India, Teachers' Day is
    observed on September 5th, commemorating the birth anniversary
    of Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan who was a distinguished scholar,
    Bharat Ratna awardee, and the first Vice President and
    second President of independent India.

    The tradition of celebrating Teachers' Day in India began in 1962,
    a country known for its rich Guru-Shishya tradition. Since its
    inception, Teachers' Day has evolved into a national tradition,
    emphasizing the respect and gratitude that society holds for teachers.
    The celebration serves as a reminder of the crucial role educators play
    in guiding and nurturing the young minds that will shape the future.
    It is a day to honor the dedication, hard work, and
    commitment of teachers in fostering a better and brighter tomorrow.

    Activites at PRJ Care Foundation on Teachers' Day:-


  • Mrs. Rajni and all the mentors were presented with cards and
    flowers by the children.

  • Mrs. Rajni shared insights about the significance of
    Teacher's Day and its importance in nation-building.

  • The celebration concluded with everyone being served refreshments,
    creating a warm and festive atmosphere.
